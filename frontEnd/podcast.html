Voices of Progress: The Speech & Language Education Podcast

Every Friday, delve into transformative discussions centered on education for children with speech and language processing challenges. "Voices of Progress" brings together renowned educators, dedicated Speech and Language Pathologists (SLPs), and groundbreaking AI specialists. Together, they explore the challenges, triumphs, and innovations in the realm of speech and language education.


Discover the incredible stories of children breaking barriers, the educators pioneering novel methodologies, and the AI technologies making timely interventions possible. Learn about the intricate workings of tools like the AI Screener and AI Orchestrator, and how they're reshaping individualized learning experiences.


Whether you're an education professional, a caregiver advocating for a child, or someone passionate about the interplay between technology and learning, our podcast offers a wealth of knowledge and inspiration. Subscribe to "Voices of Progress" and join the conversation charting a brighter, more inclusive future for all children.

National AI Institute for Exceptional Education

An NSF & IES AI Institute for Transforming Education for Children with Speech and Language Processing Challenges.


The National AI Institute for Exceptional Education is a National Artificial Intelligence (AI) Research Institute led by the University at Buffalo and funded by the National Science Foundation and the Institute of Education Sciences of the US Department of Education.
