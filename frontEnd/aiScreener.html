AI Screener

A cutting-edge solution designed for early childhood classrooms. By analyzing video and audio streams of young students' interactions, the AI Screener derives key speech and language metrics traditionally used by SLPs. This tool identifies needs early on, ensuring timely interventions and a foundation for successful communication.
