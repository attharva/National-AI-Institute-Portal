AI Orchestrator

An expansive tool primarily for public-school settings, it aids SLPs in delivering evidence-based interventions tailored to individual IEP learning targets. Built on a robust multi-agent reinforcement learning framework, the Orchestrator evaluates and suggests the most effective intervention practices, ensuring personalized strategies for every child's distinct needs.
