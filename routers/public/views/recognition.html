Stories Of Triumph

When Lia entered kindergarten, her speech and language challenges made classroom interactions a daily struggle. The introduction of the AI Screener in her classroom was a turning point. Analyzing her conversations, the tool provided tailored feedback that was instrumental in her growth. As she progressed to public school, the AI Orchestrator further fine-tuned interventions, helping her SLP pinpoint the most effective strategies. Today, Lia's confidence and communication skills have blossomed, showcasing the transformative power of technology tailored to each child's unique journey.

Empowering Every Voice: AI's Revolutionary Role in Speech and Language Education

Nearly 3.4 million children require speech and language services under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) and are at risk of falling behind in their academic and social-emotional development without timely intervention by Speech and Language Pathologists (SLPs). Unfortunately, there is a significant shortage of SLPs and the COVID pandemic has further exacerbated this gap, making it almost impossible for SLPs to provide individualized services for children.

The NSF and IES AI Institute for Transforming Education for Children with Speech and Language Processing Challenges aims to close this gap by developing advanced AI technologies to scale SLPsâ€™ availability and services such that no child in need of speech and language services is left behind.

Voices of Progress: The Speech & Language Education Podcast

Every Friday, delve into transformative discussions centered on education for children with speech and language processing challenges. "Voices of Progress" brings together renowned educators, dedicated Speech and Language Pathologists (SLPs), and groundbreaking AI specialists. Together, they explore the challenges, triumphs, and innovations in the realm of speech and language education.
