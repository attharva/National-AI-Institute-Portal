Empowering Every Voice: AI's Revolutionary Role in Speech and Language Education

Nearly 3.4 million children require speech and language services under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) and are at risk of falling behind in their academic and social-emotional development without timely intervention by Speech and Language Pathologists (SLPs). Unfortunately, there is a significant shortage of SLPs and the COVID pandemic has further exacerbated this gap, making it almost impossible for SLPs to provide individualized services for children.

The NSF and IES AI Institute for Transforming Education for Children with Speech and Language Processing Challenges aims to close this gap by developing advanced AI technologies to scale SLPsâ€™ availability and services such that no child in need of speech and language services is left behind.

Our Products

AI Screener

A cutting-edge solution designed for early childhood classrooms. By analyzing video and audio streams of young students' interactions, the AI Screener derives key speech and language metrics traditionally used by SLPs. This tool identifies needs early on, ensuring timely interventions and a foundation for successful communication.

AI Orchestrator

An expansive tool primarily for public-school settings, it aids SLPs in delivering evidence-based interventions tailored to individual IEP learning targets. Built on a robust multi-agent reinforcement learning framework, the Orchestrator evaluates and suggests the most effective intervention practices, ensuring personalized strategies for every child's distinct needs.

Bridging the Service Gap

In a world where timely intervention can define a child's future, the National AI Institute for Exceptional Education recognizes the urgency. By pioneering AI solutions, we're addressing the pressing shortage of SLPs and ensuring every child receives the personalized attention and support they deserve.

Beyond just technology, our institute brings together educators, researchers, and AI specialists. Together, we're sculpting a future where technology enhances human expertise, ensuring every child, irrespective of their challenges, thrives in an understanding environment.
